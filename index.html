英语表达方式

发表于 2017-06-03 | | 阅读次数

从读研到工作期间，我的大部分的英语都是和这个哥所学，他是居住在宾夕法尼亚洲的一个美国人。研究生毕业时的论文英文摘要在盲审的时候老师也表扬了我，说，你的英文不错嘛？ 呵呵呵，诸不知，我论文的英语摘要是这个哥帮我所写。回报是：我教他中文。此外，还有技术上的一些交流，目前他在 Penn State 读数据分析专业。爱好：打游戏，喝酒，抽烟，做炸药，anything. 我在想，还是把很多交流记录下来服务大家。

  • Why don’t you look up the definition of upon ?

  • A common phrase is “Upon closer inspection”, which is like saying “After closer inspection”.

  • Upon serialization the names of these fields are serialized = After serialization, the name of ….

  • What odd about that ? Odd is an adjective which means strange or unexpected.

  • Drinking a cold beer is so refreshing. Refreshing is an adjective in this context.

  • Do you think it’s weird too ? Oh, so just having is weird.

  • Having to deal with you is a pain in the ass John.

  • Having discharged my duty, I bid you farewell.

    Discharged 在这里的意思是 to perform a task, expecially an official one. Bid 在这里的意思是 to give a greeting to someone, or to ask someone to do someting. means 打招呼, 致歉以及请求. E.g. I must now bid you farewell which means 我现在必须向您道别了。

select split(concat_ws(',','1','2','3','4','5','6','7','8','9'), ',') from vipods.ods_log_app_ads_impression_hm where dt = '20170522' limit 9
